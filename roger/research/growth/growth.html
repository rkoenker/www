Quantile Regression Methods for Reference Growth Curves


One of the oldest applications of quantile regression ideas originating in work by Quetelet in the 1870's is the estimation of reference growth curves for children. Prior work has emphasized parametric models based on Gaussian assumptions, but nonparametric quantile regression provides a natural alternative approach.
Quantile Regression Methods for Reference Growth Curves. by Ying Wei, Anneli Pere, Roger Koenker and Xuming He.
