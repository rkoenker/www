Paper on Pennsylvania Reemployment Bonus Experiment


This is an archive of software and text for a paper titled Quantile Regression for Duration Data: A Reappraisal of the Pennsylvania Reemployment Bonus Experiments by Roger Koenker and Yannis Bilias.

A preliminary version of the paper is available here. The archive format for the paper is postscript (.ps) and can be obtained by clicking here.
or (.pdf) here. Data on which the paper is based is available in R "save" format as Penn.Rda.

Comments are, of course, always welcome. Problems or questions should be directed to roger@ysidro.econ.uiuc.edu
