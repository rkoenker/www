Censored Quantile Regression


This is an archive page for a paper titled
Censored Quantile Regression Redux

A preliminary version of the paper is available in pdf. A collection of files that can be used to reproduce the results described in the paper is available as a compressed tarball.

A note on a recent paper in Biometrical Journal by Bottai and Zhang is available pdf. and appeared in that journal in 2011. Code to reproduce the results of this note is available as a tarball.

A recent paper in with Naveen Narisetty, “Censored Quantile Regression Survival Models with a Cure Proportion” appearing in J. of Econometrics, 226, 192-203. is available here in pdf. Code to reproduce the results of this paper is available as a as a R package tarball.
