Quantile Smoothing Splines


This is an archive of software and text for the paper Quantile Smoothing Splines by Roger Koenker and Pin Ng and Stephen Portnoy. This paper has appeared in 1994 in Biometrika, 81, 673-680. A pdf version of the paper is available here converted from the original Bell Labs troff!


The archives are in tar.Z format and can be obtained by clicking here
