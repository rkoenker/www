Two Papers on Patholological Examples in Regression


This is an archive for two papers on pathological examples in regression asymptotics.

The first is The Falstaff Estimator both by Roger Koenker Jose A.F. Machado. appearing Economics Letters 61,(1998), 23-28

The second is Some Pathological Regression Asymptotics under Stable Conditions appearing in the American Statistician, (2002).
