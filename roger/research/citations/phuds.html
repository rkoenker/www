Phud Project

This page is part of my Tom Sawyer attempt to let Google OCR translate some bitmapped JEL pages into text that could then be analysed. Eventually, the idea is to study the evolution of field specialization in economics. The files linked to this page are JSTOR images of the JEL's annual list of doctoral dissertations in economics.
