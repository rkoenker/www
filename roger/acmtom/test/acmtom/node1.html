Our motivation for studying the algorithm is that it is well suited for computing the quantile smoothing splines described in Koenker, Ng, and Portnoy [2]. However, in the course of adapting the algorithm for this purpose we encountered some difficulties with the behavior of the algorithm in degenerate situations. Since our problems are extremely sparse and degeneracy is the norm rather than an exceptional situation, it proved essential to understand and correct this anomalous behavior. We describe these modifications briefly in the next section; a detailed description of the quantile smoothing spline computations will be reported elsewhere. Throughout, we adhere as closely as possible to the notation introduced by Bartels and Conn.
