Economics 478
Topics in Semiparametric Econometrics
Week 7
Roundtable Discussion of Research Topics



This week will be devoted to a continuation of discussion of research progress by participants. There will be a general discussion of research progress and

Brian Weikel will discuss work on estimation of long memory models.

I will describe some work on quantile regression based on regression depth ideas, based on recent work of Peter Rousseeuw and Mia Hubert and some ancient work of Edgeworth and Bowley. I might also mention that on Tuesday morning I'll be presenting the paper on Likelihood Ratio Processes and Quantile Regression in the Statistics Seminar (11am, Room 1, Illini Hall).
