Economics 478
Topics in Semiparametric Econometrics
Week 6
Roundtable Discussion of Research Topics



This week will be devoted to a continuation of discussion of research progress by participants. Everyone is reminded that Jim Heckman will be giving a department seminar and public lecture on thursday. These talks are a great opportunity to hear one of the leading scholars in labor and econometrics -- don't miss them!!!! Two students have been selected to present somewhat more extended descriptions of their progress:

Carrie Amidon will discuss work on decomposition of measures of inequality.

Daniel Morillo will discuss work on recursive estimation.

I will describe a problem related to bivariate splines which I am interested in solving.
