Spring 1998 Roger Koenker

Economics 478
Topics in Semiparametric Econometrics
Week 5
Roundtable Discussion of Research Topics



This week will be devoted to a discussion of research progress by participants. There will be 5 minute presentations by everyone on their topic with some motivation for the problem, some brief literature review and the plan of attack on the research remaining for the paper. Three students have been selected to present somewhat more extended descriptions of their progress:

Gregory Kordas will discuss work on binary response models.

Teruko Hippo will discuss work on Gallant's SNP density estimation methods.

Maria Tannuri will discuss work on extensions of rank based tests of the unit root hypothesis to models of dispersion.
