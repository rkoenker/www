Emma's List of Places

Here are some countries I've visited: France, Belgium, Luxembourg, Austria, Russia, Portugal, England, Australia, Czechia, Slovakia, Switzerland, Germany, Canada, Mexico
And here is a list of states I've been to: New Jersey (of course, since I was born in Princeton), California, Montana Wyoming, North and South Dakota, Minnesota, Wisconsin, Iowa, Ohio, Missouri, New York, North Carolina, West Virginia, Massachusetts, Rhode Island, Connecticutt, New Hampshire, Vermont, Maine.
In Canada I've visited the Provences of: British Columbia, Alberta, Sask, Ontario, Prince Edward Island, Nova Scotia and New Brunswick.
I am very proud to say that in all my travels, I have not been to Florida.
