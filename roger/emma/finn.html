Finn's Name Page

Finn's name was chosen because of its awesome possibilities for bad puns. Here is a famous passage from James Joyce. to illustrate this:

Hohohoho, Mister Finn, you're going to be Mister Finnagain! Comeday morm and, O, you're vine! Sendday's eve and, ah you're vinegar! Hahahaha, Mister Funn, you're going to be fined again!

Some of his nicknames are: McFinn, Finny, Finky, Flinky, Finko, Stinko, Slinky Finky, Feeno, Finners, Vinegar, Dog Face, Dork, Dogbreath, Flinny, Winky, Dorkbutt, Nose.
