Paper on Medflies


This is an archive of software and text for a paper titled Reappraising Medfly longevity: A Quantile Regression Survival Analysis by Roger Koenker and Olga Geling.

A preliminary version of the paper is available here. The archive format for the paper is postscript (.ps) and can be obtained by clicking here.
