Rank Tests for Linear Models


This is an archive for two papers on rank statistics: one is a survey paper Rank Tests for Linear Models by Roger Koenker. This paper appearing in vol 15 of The Handbook of Statistics, edited by C.R. Rao and G.S. Madalla.
